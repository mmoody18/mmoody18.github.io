Project Overview

Application Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote Javon Bennet's photography business by providing a centralized platform where clients can:

  • View his portfolio showcasing previous work and experience.
  • Access information about services offered, including rates and availability.
  • Contact Javon directly through integrated communication channels.
  • Explore details about the photography equipment used.
  • Connect with his photography Instagram feed to view the latest updates.

This online presence will serve as a professional and accessible medium for Javon to share his work and engage with potential clients and sponsors.

Intended Users

The website is designed for:

  • Potential clients seeking professional photography services.
  • Potential sponsors interested in collaborating or supporting Javon's work.
  • Individuals curious about Javon's photography and professional journey.

Website Content Overview

The website will comprise the following key sections:

  • Home Page: An inviting introduction to Javon's photography, featuring a selection of highlighted images and a brief overview of services.
  • Portfolio: A categorized gallery displaying Javon's previous work across various photography genres.
  • Services: Detailed information about the photography services offered, including pricing, packages, and availability.
  • About: Background information about Javon, his experience, and his approach to photography.
  • Contact: A user-friendly contact form for inquiries, along with links to social media profiles and other contact information.

Wireframe

Site Map

Page Design

  • Home (index.html)
    Purpose: Provide a welcoming introduction to the website and highlight key aspects of Javon's photography.
    Audience: All visitors.
    Content: Featured images, brief overview of services, and navigation links.
    Interactivity: Navigation bar linking to other pages; possibly a slideshow of featured photographs.
  • Portfolio
    Purpose: Showcase Javon's work across different photography categories.
    Audience: Potential clients and sponsors.
    Content: Categorized photo galleries with high-quality images.
    Interactivity: Clickable thumbnails that expand to full-size images in a lightbox view; category filters to sort images.
  • Services
    Purpose: Detail the photography services offered, including pricing and availability.
    Audience: Potential clients.
    Content: Descriptions of service packages, rates, booking information, and availability calendar.
    Interactivity: Buttons to book services or inquire further; possibly a dynamic calendar showing available dates.
  • About
    Purpose: Provide background information about Javon, his experience, and his photography philosophy.
    Audience: All visitors.
    Content: Biography, mission statement, and insights into Javon's approach to photography.
    Interactivity: Links to social media profiles; embedded video introduction (optional).
  • Contact
    Purpose: Facilitate communication between Javon and potential clients or collaborators.
    Audience: Potential clients, sponsors, and general inquiries.
    Content: Contact form fields for name, email, subject, and message; alternative contact methods.
    Interactivity: Form validation to ensure all required fields are completed; confirmation message upon successful submission.

Dynamic Functionality

The website will incorporate the following interactive features using JavaScript and jQuery:

  • Interactive Portfolio Gallery: Implementing a lightbox feature that allows users to click on portfolio images to view them in an enlarged overlay, enhancing the visual experience.
  • Instagram Feed Integration: Displaying the latest posts from Javon's photography Instagram account directly on the website to keep content fresh and engaging. This can be achieved by embedding the Instagram feed using available widgets or plugins.
  • Contact FormAdd a form at the end of a page to allow for people to enter their email and submit a request or contact to Javon.
